# Upgrade System Design

## Weapon Families (Phase B)

### Vulcan (Default)
- Fast fire rate, moderate damage
- Upgrade path: wider spread, faster projectiles

### Laser
- Piercing beam, slow fire rate, high damage
- Upgrade path: longer beam duration, wider beam

### Plasma
- Area damage, slow projectile speed
- Upgrade path: larger blast radius, faster reload

## Sub-Weapons

### Missile
- Lock-on homing projectile. 1 per 3 seconds.

### Bomb
- Screen-clearing super attack. 3 per mission.

### Shield
- Absorbs one hit. Recharges over time.

### Drone
- AI wingman that mirrors player movement offset. Fires independently.

## Defensive Modules

### Armor
- +2 max HP, -10% speed

### Barrier
- Energy shield that regenerates. No HP penalty.

## Upgrade Tiers (per weapon family)

| Tier | Cost  | Effect            |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| 1    | 500   | +20% fire rate    |
| 2    | 1500  | +30% damage       |
| 3    | 3000  | Special ability   |
| 4    | 5000  | Mastery (visual)  |

## Hangar Screen Flow

1. Show current loadout
2. Display available upgrades (earned from mission score)
3. Player selects one upgrade
4. Story decision prompt (if applicable)
5. Proceed to next mission
