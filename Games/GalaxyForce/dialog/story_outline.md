# Galaxy Force Story Outline

## Setting

Humanity expanded along the Halo Line -- a chain of colonized systems at the galaxy's edge.
The Seraph Dominion attacks without warning. Their motive is morally ambiguous: they believe
the Halo Line expansion threatens a dormant entity sleeping in the galactic core.

## Characters

### Kael Voss (Protagonist Pilot)
Callsign: Viper. Former cargo runner turned combat pilot. Pragmatic, dry humor.

### Ren Takara (Wing Officer)
Callsign: Ghost. By-the-book officer with a hidden compassionate streak. Relationship thread.

### Admiral Drenn (Command Authority)
Distant, strategic. Makes hard calls. May be hiding intelligence about the Dominion's motive.

### Archon Seris (Dominion Leader)
Believes genuinely in protecting the galaxy from the Halo Line expansion. Not a villain
in the traditional sense.

## Branching Points

1. **Mission 3:** Save civilian convoy (relationship +Ren) or pursue Dominion flagship (score bonus)
2. **Mission 5:** Trust Admiral Drenn's intel or investigate independently
3. **Mission 7:** Accept Archon Seris's ceasefire offer or press the attack

## Endings

### Ending A: Pyrrhic Victory
Ignore all warnings, destroy the Dominion. The dormant entity stirs. Bittersweet.

### Ending B: Uneasy Peace
Accept ceasefire, establish neutral zone. Ren relationship maxed. Hopeful.

### Ending C: Truth Revealed
Investigate independently, discover Drenn's conspiracy. Expose truth. Best ending.
